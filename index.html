04 — About

Built by Jaguar, for the floor.

UTOPIA was founded in 2020 by BBC Radio 1 DJ and producer Jaguar — a platform for the new guard of dance music. A record label releasing forward-thinking electronic music. A clubnight that's become a queer institution. A podcast that pulls back the curtain on the artists shaping the next decade of clubland.

We believe in joy, volume, and the radical act of dancing together. A perfect moment.
